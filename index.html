| About Me |

Growing up I had a passion for electronics that translated to an interest in computers and how they worked in my teens. I bought parts for and built my first computer at age 19. Up until that point I was not completely sure what I wanted to do in life. After seeing the success of my endeavors and how much I enjoyed putting it together it cemented in my mind that I had to do some sort of work with computers. Since then, I have pursued development in college and in my downtime, starting with Java and now moving onto all of the above at Digital Crafts in Atlanta, GA. During this time we were required to do all of our classes remotely, giving me agile work experience working with others over video calls. So far I have greatly enjoyed my time as a developer, and cannot wait to continue my journey creating awesome apps, sites and games in the future. Enjoy the site!
